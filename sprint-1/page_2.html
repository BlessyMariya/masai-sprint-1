WHAT IS THE PURPOSE OF GUN

  • The purpose of a gun–if we're being honest–is to kill something.
  • It's a tool that has enough power, when used properly (and very often improperly) to kill.
  • Hunting rifles and shotguns are for killing animals.
  • Handguns–if we’re being honest–are for killing people. That’s the purpose behind their design–they are portable, concealable, easy-to-use tools for killing a person.
